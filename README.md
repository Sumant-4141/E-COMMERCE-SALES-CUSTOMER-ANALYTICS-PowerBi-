# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Overview

This project presents a Power BI dashboard analyzing e-commerce sales performance, customer behavior, and regional trends. It enables businesses to monitor KPIs and derive actionable insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

How can an e-commerce company optimize sales performance, identify profitable products, and understand customer purchasing behavior?

---

## 📊 Key Metrics

* Total Sales: 484K
* Total Profit: 50K
* Total Orders: 969
* Average Order Value: 499.74

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* 📈 Sales show consistent growth across months, peaking in Q4
* 🖥 Technology category generates the highest revenue
* 🌎 Western region contributes the largest share of sales
* 🔁 Repeat customers significantly impact total revenue

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

* Power BI (Data Visualization)
* Power Query (Data Cleaning)
* DAX (Measures & KPIs)
